Inter-School JAIPURIA Fest ~

We welcome you to the marvelous Inter-School Jaipuria fest, a three-day fun-filled vivacious event filled with once-in-a-lifetime opportunities for students to engross themselves in captivating and enthralling events. The three happening days of this fest will include:-
1st Day- Meraki
2nd Day- Yogoutsav & Ciencia
3rd Day- Techvista & Bhavani Bharti
This event will not only cater to students' enjoyment but also form an integral part of their learnings that will come useful to them throughout their entire life. As it is said- "Learning is a treasure that will follow its owner everywhere."
