Inter-School JAIPURIA Fest ~

We welcome you to the marvelous Inter-School Jaipuria fest, a three-day fun-filled vivacious event filled with once-in-a-lifetime opportunities for students to engross themselves in captivating and enthralling events. The three happening days of this fest will include:-
1st Day- Meraki
2nd Day- Yogoutsav & Ciencia
3rd Day- Techvista & Bhavani Bharti
This event will not only cater to students' enjoyment but also form an integral part of their learnings that will come useful to them throughout their entire life. As it is said- "Learning is a treasure that will follow its owner everywhere."

Meraki

Meraki invites students to showcase their dormant talents with the same zeal and virtuosity, similar to last year. It includes fantastic literary and mathematical events that give students a once in a lifetime opportunity to exhibit their talents and learn whilst they participate. Meraki will not only have intellect in store for for students but also inquisitiveness. It is all about enthusiasm to participate and learnings imparted. It is just one step away, get registered!

Yogoutsav

Yoga boasts its nonchalence and with its suavity, boosts the yogi's intellect, flexibility and overall resilience. Yogoutsav celebrates the brilliance of yoga and its astounding benefits. This event will rouse student's interest for the fabulous art of yoga.

Bhavani Bharati

Sanskrit is the language of scientists and intellect. In ancient times, this language was used as a means of communication and dialogue by the Hindu Celestial Gods. It requires dedication, hard work and open-mindedness. Bhavani bharati, with its shlok chantings will improve students' sanskrit diction and pronunciation while inculcating rational thoughts in their minds.

Read Brochure


Techvista

Today, in the absence of technology, our life comes at an inert standstill. Technology helps us to communicate and deal with the dynamic things of our day to day life. Techvista illustrates our bond with technology in a zealous and pleasurable way. It encompasses several IT related competitions including quizzes, coding and debugging. The Space odyssey and futuristic themed event will boost students' knowledge about technology and cater to their inquisitiveness while learning.
